Lesson Plans that help you teach children effectly.

Chuwei, March 2016

I have been teaching children for years and found out many of my students' parents have troubles teaching their children at home.

These are the things I have heard from the parents:

  • "My kid cannot stay focused."
  • "He just wouldn't listen to me."
  • "This is the fourth time I teach XXX, but she still cannot get it."
  • "I don't know how to teach my kid. It's always a battle."

A good lesson plan would greatly reduce the time and effort on your part, and helps your children retain information much longer. An effective lesson plan has five steps: Hook, Teach, Practice, Accessment, Closure.
